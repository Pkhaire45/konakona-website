Our Services

Transforming spaces into stunning works of art is our passion. At Konakona interiors, we specialize in creating interiors that reflect your personality, enhance functionality, and elevate aesthetics. From cozy residential spaces to dynamic commercial environments, our team brings creativity, expertise, and attention to detail to every project we undertake

We believe that every space has a story waiting to be told, and we’re here to bring it to life. Whether you're looking to redesign a single room, renovate an entire home, or create a unique ambiance for your office or retail space, our approach blends style and practicality seamlessly. Using the finest materials, the latest trends, and timeless design principles, we ensure your vision becomes a reality.

OUR PROFESSIONAL

SERVICES

Residential Interior Design

Your home is your sanctuary, and we’re here to make it a place of comfort, beauty, and functionality. Whether you prefer minimalist, rustic, or luxurious styles, our residential design services cater to every taste and budget. We handle everything from conceptualization to sourcing materials and installing fixtures, leaving you with a stunning, move-in-ready space.

Commercial Interior Design

In today’s competitive world, a well-designed workplace can make all the difference. Our commercial interior design services focus on creating functional, attractive spaces that align with your brand and business goals. From offices and retail stores to hospitality venues, we design environments that inspire employees and captivate customers.

Space Planning

Maximizing your space is crucial for comfort and efficiency. We provide tailored space planning solutions that consider your needs and optimize each square foot. Through careful layout and strategic furniture placement, we ensure that every area serves a purpose and enhances your overall experience.

Architecture Services

Our architecture services focus on crafting innovative, functional, and aesthetically pleasing spaces that reflect your vision. We provide customized architectural designs for residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects, ensuring every structure is both beautiful and practical. From concept to completion, our approach integrates sustainable design principles and cutting-edge technology, creating spaces that enhance the user experience while maximizing efficiency, comfort, and style.

Exterior Interior Design

Our exterior design services focus on creating visually appealing, functional, and durable exteriors for residential and commercial properties. From innovative landscaping and façades to lighting and outdoor spaces, we design with an emphasis on enhancing curb appeal and ensuring that every detail aligns with the aesthetic and practical needs of your space. Whether it's modern, traditional, or eco-friendly, we tailor designs to reflect your style while ensuring long-term sustainability and value.

Other Services

In addition to our core offerings, we provide a wide range of complementary services to support your design and construction needs. These include project consultation, 3D modeling, space planning, construction management, and more. Our team works closely with you to deliver tailored solutions that meet your specific requirements, ensuring every detail is handled with care and expertise. Whether you're looking for guidance on a new build, need help with renovations, or require specialized design assistance, we’re here to help you bring your ideas to life.
