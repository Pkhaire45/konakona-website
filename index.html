The

Process

Our goal is to clearly outline the process of collaborating with our clients. We strive to ensure that the workflow is seamless, reducing any stress or challenges associated with the design process. Our commitment is to deliver a final product that perfectly reflects the vision and expectations shared by the client.

1. Initial Client Engagement

  • Conducting an meeting and confirming the lead.
  • Discussing and finalizing design requirements.
  • Estimating project costs and timelines.
  • Reviewing legal considerations and compliance.

2. BOQ & Timeline

  • Create a comprehensive Bill of Quantities (BOQ).
  • Confirm the project commencement date.
  • Place orders for essential materials in advance.
  • Plan and finalize execution strategies.

3. Site Preparation

  • Begin delivery of required materials.
  • Assign and deploy skilled workforce.
  • Establish effective coordination mechanisms.
  • Set up internal monitoring systems.

4. Project Handover

  • Complete all site tasks.
  • Address and resolve any outstanding issues.
  • Conduct final inspection and handover the project.
  • Issue a completion certificate.
